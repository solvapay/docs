## Sign Up

1. Navigate to the [SolvaPay Console](https://app.solvapay.com)
2. Click **Sign Up** or **Get Started**
3. Choose your sign-in method:
   - **Continue with Google** - Use your Google account
   - **Continue with GitHub** - Use your GitHub account

No passwords to remember—authentication is handled through your existing Google or GitHub account.

## Complete Your Profile

After signing in for the first time, you'll be prompted to complete your provider profile:

1. **Provider Name** - Your business or brand name (displayed to customers)
2. **Contact Email** - Where you'll receive important notifications

## Console Overview

Once signed in, you'll see the main dashboard with these key areas:

| Section | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Dashboard** | Overview of usage, revenue, and key metrics |
| **Products** | Create and manage products, plans, and integration options |
| **MCP Pay** | Configure hosted MCP monetization for products (no-code option) |
| **Developers** | Access SDK integration settings and developer workflows |
| **Plans** | Create and manage pricing plans |
| **Customers** | View and manage your customers |
| **Settings** | Configure branding, payments, and account settings |

## Connect a Payment Provider

Before you can accept payments, connect your Stripe account:

1. Go to **Settings > Payments**
2. Click **Connect Stripe**
3. Follow the Stripe onboarding flow
4. Once connected, you can create paid plans

## Configure Your Branding

Customize how your brand appears on customer-facing pages:

1. Go to **Settings > Pages**
2. Upload your **logo**
3. Set your **brand name**
4. Choose an **accent color**
5. Select a **font family**
6. Configure **light/dark mode** color palettes

Your branding is automatically applied to login pages, checkout, and the customer portal.
